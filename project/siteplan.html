Site Plan: Introduction to Bioinformatics

Site Name

Domain: intro-bioinformatics.org (example)

This name clearly represents the educational and introductory purpose of the site, which is focused on Bioinformatics, a multidisciplinary science combining biology, computer science, and data analysis.

Site Purpose

This website is an informational hub for students and enthusiasts who are new to bioinformatics. It introduces key concepts, showcases important tools and programming languages, and provides an interactive sequence analyzer to demonstrate a real-world application of the subject.

Scenarios

  • What are the most common tools used in bioinformatics, and what are they used for?
  • Can I analyze a DNA sequence to detect CRISPR targets using a simple online tool?

Color Scheme

  • #1abc9c (Turquoise): Primary color for navigation bars, headings, buttons.
  • #2c3e50 (Dark Blue Gray): Used for text headings and section borders.
  • #f4f4f9 (Light Gray): Background color to enhance readability and contrast.

Typography

  • Segoe UI: Used for body text and paragraphs for clear readability.
  • Roboto Slab (optional): May be used for headings or cards to give a modern, professional look.
